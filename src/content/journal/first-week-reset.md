This blog is my personal space for writing things down before they disappear.

I wanted something simple, fast, and focused. Not a heavy portfolio, not a content machine, and not a template that looks like everyone else's site.

## Purpose

The purpose of this blog is to keep a useful record of:

- projects I am building
- things I learn
- ideas worth revisiting
- experiments, notes, and small updates
- occasional personal writing

I want it to be lightweight enough that posting feels easy.

## Style

The design is intentionally minimal, but it has personality.

I chose a Linux terminal-inspired UI because it feels close to how I actually work: text-first, focused, and a little playful. The goal is not to mimic a terminal perfectly, but to make the site feel like a small app window for notes.

That means:

- monospace typography
- terminal-like panels and prompts
- dark theme with green/cyan accents
- clean structure and readable content

## What to expect here

Posts can be technical, personal, or somewhere in between.

This is a place for documenting process, not just polished outcomes.
